Gradescope Average

This website is a quick tool to calculate your average grades on Gradescope.

  1. Sign in to https://www.gradescope.com/
  2. Navigate to the gradescope website of your class
  3. Press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy all of the website's text to your clipboard
  4. Return to this website, press Ctrl+V to paste the text into the textarea
  5. Press the "Calculate Grade" button to calculate your average grade!

We did not find any grades in your input, please try again with different input. Make sure you are following the instructions properly.

Note: This is a third-party website that is not officially affiliated or maintained by Gradescope. As of May 2024 this website is able to correctly parse Gradescope's website, although it may need an update in the future.
